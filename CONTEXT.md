# Calltrainer

An AI-powered trainer that helps users improve telephone communication skills through simulated conversations, speech analysis, and qualitative feedback on speaking behavior over time.

## Language

**Session**:
One full pass a user makes through the product: a simulated phone conversation followed by feedback on their communication behavior.
_Avoid_: Call, conversation, training session

**User**:
The person who performs Sessions, practicing their telephone communication skills against an AI-simulated conversation partner. There is no separate human trainer/coach role — feedback is generated by the AI.
_Avoid_: Trainee, learner, customer

**Scenario**:
The situational context configured for a Session — e.g. the caller's reason for calling or goal. Independent of the simulated counterpart's character traits (see Persona).
_Avoid_: Persona, situation

**Persona**:
The character traits of the AI-simulated conversation partner within a Session — e.g. impatient, calm, confrontational. Combined independently with a Scenario.
_Avoid_: Scenario, character, counterpart

**Feedback**:
Qualitative, behavior-focused insight generated by the AI — either scoped to a single Session or aggregated across a User's Sessions over time to surface recurring communication patterns. One term covers both scopes; the scope is a property of a given piece of Feedback, not a separate concept.
_Avoid_: Score, evaluation, result
